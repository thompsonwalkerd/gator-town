What is Gator Town?

Gator Town is a store for students and teachers to buy school themed items to show school spirit. There are many items up for sale including hats, t-shirts, hoodies, water bottles, and much more. Having a school store is very unique and very beneficial for funding various school activites. Gator Town is a room the Learning Commons, however students are able to access the store through a window located on mainstreet. Business students are also benefitd by the store because they can work in the store and learn how retailer work. Many students visit Gator Town to get snacks, seasonal items, water bottoles, and apparel to show school spirit.


History

Gator Town was envisioned by Dr. Nute in 2012, and the idea was pitched to Dr. Luke Clamp. Dr. Clamp loved the idea, and when the school opened in 2013, Gator Town was born. The location of Gator Town was originaly a confrence room, however Dr. Clamp allowed Dr. Nute to convert it into a school store. All of the money would be put into the DECA program at the school.
